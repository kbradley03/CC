Bio

The Creative Cypher is a conscientious community of innovative directors, producers, screenwriters, film editors, sound engineers, composers and performers, founded in 2013 by Troy Pryor. 501(c)(3)

Mission

To be a resource and support system for its membership. To provide mentoring and training to those interested in a career in the entertainment industry.

Vision

Through guidance and leadership the goal of Creative Cypher is to broaden access, expand opportunity, and increase diversity in the general Chicago creative community.
